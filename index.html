ABOUT

The importance of engineers in our society cannot be understated. The contributions they make to build the society with the tools at their disposal is something that is often understated.
Engineering Summit, a national level summit, is a conference to teach participants what it takes to become a real engineer working in the industry. With prominent personalities in their respective fields talking about the latest cutting edge technologies, this summit is sure to enlighten you on the variety of prospects of engineering. There will be a general track followed by multiple track sessions and finally a mentoring session, where participants get to interact with top minds from many different fields.

General Track

Hear the top minds in the society talk about how important the field really is in this modern day and age from their multitude of experiences. To those who hold doubts about the role engineering plays in shaping our future, this session will open your eyes to the great possibilities that lie ahead.
This is a golden opportunity to gain insight from the brightest minds in the field of engineering. For our speakers will remove all doubts and will voice their opinions on just how integral engineering is for a progressive future. As a result, Engineering Summit hopes to invoke the engineer in you and help you prepare to be part of a positive change in the future of humanity.

The Future of Consumption
(track 1)

The future of retail is personalisation. Listen to this enthralling talk about how our future in retail will be invisible and omnipresent, with simpler transactions and no storefronts, which will make the act of buying a seamless part of contemporary life.

Nuclear Energy
(track 3)

For decades, atomic energy has been shrouded in a deep mist of misconceptions, fueled by Soviet-era horror films. Learn about how nuclear energy embodies the saying, "Small is beautiful" and how nuclear energy is the way towards a greener future.

Speed Mentoring

A unique platform where the participants can interact with multiple mentors within a given set of time. Seek expert advice or have an enlightening talk with our highly experienced mentor panel.
With a panel of 10 mentors, the event consists of different sessions, where the participants are grouped into 10 teams, with 20 members in each team
In the first session, each of these teams will get to interact with each of the mentors within the time limit of 10-15 minutes. After this, the teams will swap their mentors and swapping continues until all the teams cover each of the 10 mentors.
