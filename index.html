Engineering Summit

One Liner about the event

ABOUT

The importance of engineers in our society cannot be understated. The contributions they make to building the society with the tools at their disposal is something that is often understated.
Engineering Summit, a national level summit, is a conference to teach participants what it takes to become a real engineer working in the industry. With prominent personalities in their respective fields, this summit is sure to enlighten you on the varied prospects of engineering. There will be a general track followed by a multiple track session and finally a mentoring session, where participants get to interact with top minds from many different fields.

General Track

Hear the top minds in the society talk about how important the field really is in this modern day and age from their multitude of experiences. To those who hold doubts about the role engineering plays in shaping our future, this session will open your eyes to the great possibilities that lie ahead.
This is a golden opportunity to gain insight from the brightest minds in the field of engineering. For our speakers will remove all doubts and will voice their opinions on just how integral engineering is for a progressive future. As a result, Engineering Summit hopes to invoke the engineer in you and help you prepare to be part of a positive change in the future of humanity.

Speed Mentoring

A unique platform where the participants can interact with multiple mentors within a given set of time. Seek expert advice or have an enlightening talk with our highly experienced mentor panel.
With a panel of 10 mentors, the event constitute different sessions, where the participants are grouped into 10 teams, with 20 members in each team
In the first session, each of these teams will get to interact with each of the mentors within the time limit of 10-15 minutes. After this, the teams will swap their mentors and swapping continues until all the teams cover each of the 10 mentors.
